FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does this extension collect my data?
No. All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. The extension is fully privacy-first.
Will my changes persist after I close the browser?
Yes. All hide, blur, randomize, and word rule effects are saved locally and restored automatically when you revisit a page.
Can I undo changes?
Yes. Press Ctrl+Z (or Cmd+Z on Mac) to undo the last action. You can also remove individual effects from the popup or dashboard.
Which browsers are supported?
Element Hider Pro works on Chrome, Firefox, and Microsoft Edge. It's built with Manifest V3 and follows cross-browser standards.
Can I export and share my rules?
Yes. Use the Export button in the popup or dashboard to download a JSON file. You can import it on another browser or share it with others.
Does it work on dynamic pages (SPAs)?
Yes. The extension uses a MutationObserver to detect new content and automatically re-applies your rules to dynamically loaded elements.
