Philosophy

Built with
intention.

01

Local by default

Every extension stores data in your browser only. No servers, no sync, no cloud. Your data never leaves your machine.

02

Zero dependencies

Hand-written HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no bundlers, no node_modules. Ship nothing you don't need.

03

Cross-browser native

Built on Manifest V3 and web standards. One codebase runs on Chrome, Firefox, and Edge without modification.

04

Open and free

No paywalls, no premium tiers, no accounts. Every extension is free forever. If it's useful, it should be accessible.
