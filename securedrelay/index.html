FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is an email alias?
An email alias is a unique forwarding address that delivers emails to your real inbox. Instead of giving out your real email, you use the alias. If it gets compromised, you can disable or delete it without affecting your actual address.
Where do emails sent to my alias go?
All emails sent to your alias are automatically forwarded to the real email address associated with your SecuredRelay account. The sender never sees your real address.
Can I stop using an alias?
Yes. You can disable an alias to stop forwarding temporarily, or delete it permanently. Any emails sent to a deleted alias will be rejected.
Is the extension free?
Yes, SecuredRelay is free to install and use. Create an unlimited number of aliases and manage them all from your browser.
Does the extension collect my browsing data?
No. SecuredRelay does not collect browsing history, search queries, or any analytics. It only communicates with the SecuredRelay API for alias management. See our Privacy Policy for details.
